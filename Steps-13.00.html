Create the Card 13

To Create the Total Cases Card is very similar to creating the slicers.

STEP 1: Create # Of Cases card.

1.   Double click on the Card Icon on the visualization bar.

2.   Card visual will display on the report

3.   Drag and drop the "# Of Cases" column from the Foreign Workers table.



STEP 2: Configure the Format.

1.   Click on the Format icon

2.   Click General Tab to fix the Card Size: See screenshot below

3.   Fix the Data Label:     Display Units: Thousands   Text size:  30

4.   Turn Category: Off

5.   Turn Title: On

6.   Type "Total Cases"

7.   Turn Border: On       Color: White, 10% darker      Radius:10
