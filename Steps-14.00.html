Create the KPIs 14

Now it is time to create the KPIs; these are the steps to follow.

STEP 1: Create Denied Cases card and YOY card.

1.   Double click on the Card Icon On the visualization bar.

2.   Card visual will display on the report

3.   Drag and drop the ".Denied Cases" column from the Foreign Workers table.

4.   Repeat steps 1 to 3 to create the1 to 3 YOY Card.



STEP 2: Configure the "Denied Cases" and YOY Visuals

1.   Click on the Format icon.

2.   Click on the General Tab to fix Card Size: See screenshot below

3.   Fix the Data Label:     Text size: 30    Font Family: Trebuchet MS

4.   Turn the Category: Off

5.   Turn the title: On

6.   Type "Denied Cases".

7.   Turn the border: On    Color: White, 10% darker      Radius:10.

8.   Select the "Denied Cases" Column.

9.   Change the Format to:       Percentage      Select the "0" decimal places.

10.  Repeat steps 1 to 9 for the YOY Card.     Select 2 decimals places.
