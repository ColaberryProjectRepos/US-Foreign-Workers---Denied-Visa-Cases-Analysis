Create the Slicers 12

STEP 1: Create Year Slicer and City Slicer.

1.     Double click on the Slicer Icon On the visualization bar.

2.     Slicer visual will display on the report.

3.     Drag and drop the Year column from the calendar table.

4.     Repeat steps 1 to 3 to create the City slicer.



STEP 2:

1.   Click on the drop-down button from the title header 

2.   Select Drop down Option of the City slicer.

3.   Turn the header: Off     Change the Items    Font color: black   Font Family: Arial Black   Font Size: 10.

4.   Turn the title: On

5.   Type "City".

6.   Turn on the border.      Color: White, 10% darker      radius:  10.

7.   Fix the Slicer Size: See screenshots below.

8.   Repeat steps 3 to 7 for the Year Slicer.  Click on Numeric Inputs in place of Items.
